🎉 The Day I Was Born

I was born on November 16, 2007, which was a Friday. Below you can find what was going on in the world around that time, my horoscope, a little about my family, and some of my personality traits.

⭐ Horoscope: Scorpio ♏

Since I was born on November 16, my sign is Scorpio. Scorpios are known for being determined, passionate, and resourceful. They don't give up easily and always try to find answers. I think that fits me pretty well, especially with climbing and coding.

👪 Family

I grew up in a close family in Wisconsin. My parents always pushed me to stay curious and work hard. Being around people who valued education and putting in effort definitely shaped who I am today.

🌎 What Was Happening on November 16, 2007?

A lot was going on when I was born. The iPhone had just come out a few months before in June 2007, which pretty much changed everything about technology. Hollywood was in the middle of the WGA writers' strike, so most TV shows stopped production. Mass Effect, the video game, also came out that same month. Facebook had just opened to everyone the year before and social media was really just getting started. Kanye West, Rihanna, and Timbaland were big in music at the time. The U.S. economy was also starting to head toward the 2008 financial crisis. It was a pretty interesting time to be born.

🧠 My Personality Traits

I would say I am pretty curious and analytical. I like figuring out how things work, whether that's a climbing problem, a coding bug, or something from class. I don't really give up when things get hard. My teammates would probably say I stay calm and keep a positive attitude even when things aren't going well. I am also pretty persistent. If I get stuck on something I will keep coming back to it until I figure it out. More than anything I just really like learning new things, especially when it comes to technology.

🔬 Personality Test Result: INTP 💻

Based on the personality test, I got INTP which is called "The Thinker." INTPs are logical and analytical and like digging into complex ideas and systems. They tend to be independent and curious. I think this fits me well because I always want to understand why something works, not just that it works.
