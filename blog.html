    🌎 Places I've Traveled

    I've been lucky enough to travel to some incredible places. Dubai blew me away with its skyline and energy — unlike anywhere I've ever been. India holds a special personal meaning, connecting me with family and culture. Cancun was a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure. And Sedona, Arizona is stunning — the red rock landscapes are the kind of thing that makes you stop and just appreciate the world around you.
